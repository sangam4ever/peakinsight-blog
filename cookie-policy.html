Cookie Policy

Understand how we use cookies to enhance your experience

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer. They contain information about your browsing patterns. Most major websites use cookies to provide useful features to their users. Cookies help us understand how you use our website so we can improve it.

By using this website, you consent to the placement of cookies in your browser in accordance with this policy. If you do not wish to accept cookies, you can adjust your browser settings, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes including:

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To understand how visitors use our website
  • To deliver targeted advertising content
  • To provide a personalized browsing experience
  • To analyze website performance and usage patterns
  • To prevent fraud and improve security
  • To track conversions and campaign effectiveness

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas of the website, and payment processing. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly, and you cannot complete transactions or access essential features. These cookies are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website. These cookies help us analyze patterns and trends in user behavior, which allows us to improve the performance and usability of our site. Google Analytics is one of the primary analytics tools we use. The information collected includes the number of visits, the pages viewed, and the duration of visits.

Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver targeted advertising content based on your interests and browsing behavior. They help us understand which advertisements are effective and allow advertisers to show you relevant ads. These cookies may track your activity across multiple websites. Third-party ad networks may use this information to serve you advertisements on other websites that match your interests.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enhance the website experience by remembering your choices and preferences. These cookies allow us to provide enhanced functionality and customization. For example, they may remember your username or your region, so you don't need to re-enter this information when you return to the website.

Third-Party Cookies

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page at https://www.google.com/settings/ads.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your browsing patterns, including the pages you visit, the links you click, and the amount of time you spend on each page. This information helps us improve our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How to Control Cookies

Most modern browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in your browser's preferences or privacy settings. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only certain types of cookies
  • Decline all non-essential cookies
  • Delete cookies that have already been stored

Please note that if you disable cookies, you may not be able to access all features of our website, and your browsing experience may be limited.

Your continued use of our website following any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
